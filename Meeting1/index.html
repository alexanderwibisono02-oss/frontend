Clothes

Clothes are items worn on the body, typically made of fabric, designed for protection, warmth, modesty, or decoration. They include garments like shirts, pants, and dresses. Synonyms include clothing, apparel, attire, garb, garments, and toggery. They are essential for daily life, covering, and self-expression.
